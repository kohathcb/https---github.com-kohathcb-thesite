Welcome to the Agency

The Paper Bag Agents are 500 1 of 1 collectible game characters developed by Underground Labs Studios. Agents are the secret dimesion traveling protectors of the metaverse they come in various species, bags, outfits, backgrounds, and statuses some more rare than others. Each agent will be your access pass to games and projects developed by The Agency. The Paper Bag Agents will have a mint price of 0.1 Sol.

F.A.Q

How much will it cost to mint ?

For 0.1 $Sol with 20.0% royalties

How many Agents can I mint ?

There is no limit to how many but to give everyone a chance you will only be able to mint 1 at a time

What is Paper Racers ?

Paper Racers is the first game being developed for the PBA collectibles. Paper Racer is a Virtual Reality arcade kart racer for agents to compete with each other to see whos the fastest on the track. Paper Racer will have play to earn tournaments and challenges hosted by The Agency and other community members for a chance for players to win additional tokens, NFTs, and other Agency and Under Ground Lab merch.

When is the Full Paper Racers release ?

Paper Racers Alpha build is set to showcase on 4/20 of 2022 with its full release following behind in Q1 of 2023.

What does a Paper Bag Agent come with ?

At launch each Paper Agent comes with a high resolution 2048x2048 .PNG of your agent. Secures your Claim of your agents 3D voxelated avatar , Monthly Agent Claims, and access to all future projects in the The Agency and Underground Labs Studio Metaverses as OG collectors.
